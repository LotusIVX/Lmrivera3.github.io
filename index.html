My name is Lindsey and I'm originally from Cleveland Ohio. I moved to North Carolina in May of 2012 with my now Husband Danny, in search of better work opportunities. We started out in Cary then migrated to Durham before eventailly settling down in Louisburg. We moved here right as the pandemic began in April 2020 and made friends with some of the neighbors, started a salsa garden and eventually started venturing out when things began to open up. One really cool thing that the town does during the summer is "Friday Nights on the Tar", which is a summer concert series hosted once a month at the ampitheater.

I'm one of six siblings, one biological sister, one biological step sister (I've never actually met her) and three step sisters by marriage. My dad and step mom were the first to make it to North Carolina, followed by my closest step sister, Kylie, then myself. The rest of our family still lives up north in different parts of Ohio.
